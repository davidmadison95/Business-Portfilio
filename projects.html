Mini Roguelike Game

A browser-based mini roguelike game featuring turn-based combat, enemy AI, and procedural elements. Players navigate through a dungeon, battle enemies, collect health potions, and try to reach the exit while managing their health and turn count.

Key Features:

  • Turn-based movement and combat system
  • Responsive design with mobile touch controls
  • Simple AI for enemy movement and combat
  • High score tracking with local storage
  • Sound effects using Web Audio API
  • Swipe gestures for mobile gameplay
